## Executive Summary

StartBid is an always-on auction marketplace targeting 10x returns for seed investors through a $5M raise at $25M post-money valuation. The company has proven traction with 148k registrations, 88% bid participation, and $5.5M trailing GMV on legacy rails with no marketing tech. The investment thesis hinges on migrating to an AI-driven platform capable of 5M+ auctions/year with 6-second closing cycles.

**Verdict: CONDITIONAL PASS** - Deal math works under conservative assumptions; key diligence on team credentials and technical scalability required.
