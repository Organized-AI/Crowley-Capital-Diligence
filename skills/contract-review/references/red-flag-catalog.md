# Red Flag Catalog

## Investment Document Red Flags

### Veto-Level (Do Not Proceed)

| Red Flag | Why It's Bad | What to Do |
|----------|--------------|------------|
| **Participating preferred (uncapped)** | Investors get preference AND pro-rata equity | Negotiate cap or convert to non-participating |
| **Full ratchet anti-dilution** | Any down round = massive founder dilution | Require broad-based weighted average |
| **Redemption rights at investor option** | Creates cash obligation on demand | Remove or make mutual |
| **Unlimited protective provisions** | Investor can block any action | Negotiate standard list |
| **Founder vesting reset without acceleration** | All founder equity restarts at 0% | Negotiate partial credit or acceleration |
| **Pay-to-play with punitive conversion** | Converts preferred to common if don't participate | Negotiate to common-like preferred |

### High Concern (Negotiate Hard)

| Red Flag | Why It's Bad | Acceptable Alternative |
|----------|--------------|------------------------|
| **2x+ liquidation preference** | Requires 2x+ return before common sees anything | 1x non-participating |
| **Super pro-rata rights** | Forces company to give investor extra allocation | Standard pro-rata only |
| **Most favored nation on future rounds** | Locks in terms that may not fit | Time-limited MFN |
| **Broad definition of change of control** | Triggers unwanted provisions | Narrow to M&A events |
| **Founder salary caps without board approval** | Limits founder compensation | Reasonable limits with adjustment process |

### Notable (Document & Monitor)

| Red Flag | Why It's Bad | Mitigation |
|----------|--------------|------------|
| Cumulative dividends | Accrues regardless of distributions | Ensure accrual is reasonable |
| Broad information rights | May include sensitive operational data | Define scope clearly |
| Observer rights for all investors | Too many voices in board | Limit to lead investor |

## Customer Agreement Red Flags

### Revenue Quality Concerns

| Red Flag | Impact on Valuation | Mitigation |
|----------|---------------------|------------|
| **>25% from one customer** | Concentration risk, lower multiple | Diversification plan required |
| **<12 month contracts** | Unpredictable revenue | Push for multi-year deals |
| **Termination for convenience** | Revenue could disappear | Require cause or penalty |
| **No auto-renewal** | Must re-sell every contract | Add auto-renewal clause |
| **Quarterly billing** | Less cash flow predictability | Move to annual prepay |

### Liability Concerns

| Red Flag | Risk Level | Standard |
|----------|------------|----------|
| **Unlimited liability** | Existential risk | Cap at 12 months fees |
| **Uncapped indemnity** | Existential risk | Cap at insurance + fees |
| **No limitation on consequential damages** | Unpredictable exposure | Mutual exclusion |

### Competitive Concerns

| Red Flag | Impact | Response |
|----------|--------|----------|
| **Exclusivity clauses** | Blocks market expansion | Time-limit or narrow scope |
| **MFN on pricing** | Can't offer better deals | Limit to same customer class |
| **Non-compete in customer's industry** | Limits total addressable market | Remove or narrow |

## Corporate Document Red Flags

### IP Issues

| Red Flag | Why It's Critical | Resolution Required |
|----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Missing founder IP assignment** | Company doesn't own core IP | Get assignment immediately |
| **Missing contractor assignments** | Key code may not be owned | Retroactive assignments |
| **Open source contamination** | GPL code in proprietary product | Legal audit required |
| **Prior employer IP claims** | Founder may have restrictions | Review prior employment |

### Employment Issues

| Red Flag | Risk | Mitigation |
|----------|------|------------|
| **Key person no non-compete** | Can leave and compete | Get non-compete (where enforceable) |
| **Founder vesting complete** | No retention mechanism | New grants or acceleration provisions |
| **No IP assignment in employment agreements** | Inventions may not transfer | Update agreements |
| **At-will with no notice** | Key people can leave immediately | Add notice periods |

### Equity Issues

| Red Flag | Problem | Solution |
|----------|---------|----------|
| **83(b) elections not filed** | Massive tax liability on vesting | Cannot fix retroactively - assess exposure |
| **Option pool exhausted** | Cannot hire without dilution | Plan pool increase in round |
| **Unclear cap table** | Ownership disputes | Full capitalization reconciliation |
| **Outstanding convertible notes** | Hidden dilution | Model fully-diluted impact |
