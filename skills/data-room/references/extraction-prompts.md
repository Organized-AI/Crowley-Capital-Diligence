# Document Extraction Prompts

Standard questions for extracting key information from due diligence documents.

## Financial Documents

### P&L / Income Statement
- What is the total revenue for each period?
- What is the gross profit and gross margin?
- What are the major operating expense categories?
- What is the net income or loss?
- What is the revenue growth rate period over period?

### Balance Sheet
- What is the current cash position?
- What are the total assets and liabilities?
- What is the debt-to-equity ratio?
- Are there any significant receivables or payables?

### Cash Flow Statement
- What is the operating cash flow?
- What is the burn rate?
- What are the major uses of cash?
- What is the runway based on current burn?

## Legal Documents

### Customer Contracts
- What are the payment terms?
- What is the contract duration?
- Are there auto-renewal provisions?
- What are the termination conditions?
- What is the liability cap?
- Are there any exclusivity clauses?

### Vendor Agreements
- What services are being provided?
- What is the pricing structure?
- Are there minimum commitments?
- What are the SLA terms?

### Employment Agreements
- Are there non-compete clauses?
- What are the vesting schedules?
- Are there change of control provisions?
- What are the severance terms?

## Cap Table Documents

### Capitalization Table
- What is the current ownership breakdown?
- What is the option pool size and allocation?
- What shares are outstanding vs authorized?
- Are there any convertible instruments?

### Previous Funding Rounds
- What were the terms of each round?
- What are the liquidation preferences?
- Are there any anti-dilution provisions?
- What is the participation status of preferred?

## Customer Documents

### CRM Export
- Who are the top 10 customers by revenue?
- What is the customer count by segment?
- What is the average contract value?
- What is the customer acquisition timeline?

### Churn Analysis
- What is the monthly/annual churn rate?
- What are the primary reasons for churn?
- Are there patterns in churned customers?

## Product Documents

### Roadmap
- What are the key planned features?
- What is the development timeline?
- What are the technical dependencies?

### Architecture
- What is the tech stack?
- What are the scalability considerations?
- Are there any technical debt concerns?
