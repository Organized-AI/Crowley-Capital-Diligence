# Risk Assessment Framework

Systematic startup evaluation using the Tunguz 11-risks framework.

## The 11 Risks

| # | Risk Category | Core Question | Weight |
|---|--------------|---------------|--------|
| 1 | Market Timing | Is the market ready now? | 5% |
| 2 | Business Model | Can this make money sustainably? | 12% |
| 3 | Market Adoption | Will customers actually buy? | 10% |
| 4 | Market Size | Is the opportunity big enough? | 12% |
| 5 | Execution | Can the team ship and scale? | 10% |
| 6 | Technology | Is there defensible tech advantage? | 8% |
| 7 | Capitalization | Is funding sufficient and efficient? | 8% |
| 8 | Competition | Can they win against alternatives? | 8% |
| 9 | Team | Are these the right people? | 15% |
| 10 | Regulatory/Legal | Are there compliance landmines? | 5% |
| 11 | Exit Potential | Can this return the fund? | 7% |

## Scoring

| Score | Rating | Definition |
|-------|--------|------------|
| 9-10 | Exceptional | Clear strength, competitive advantage |
| 7-8 | Strong | Above average, low risk |
| 5-6 | Acceptable | Average, manageable risk |
| 3-4 | Concerning | Below average, requires mitigation |
| 1-2 | Critical | Major risk, potential deal-breaker |

## Veto Rules

**Automatic Pass if:**
- Team score < 3
- Market size score < 4
- Business model score < 3
- Any score = 1

## Recommendation Thresholds

| Overall Score | Recommendation |
|--------------|----------------|
| 8.0+ | Strong conviction, lead the round |
| 7.0-7.9 | Positive, participate in round |
| 6.0-6.9 | Cautious positive, need risk mitigation |
| 5.0-5.9 | Pass, but monitor |
| <5.0 | Clear pass |
