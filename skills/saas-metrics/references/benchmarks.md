## Red Flag Thresholds

| Metric | Red Flag | Investigation |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| LTV:CAC | <2.0 | Unit economics broken |
| Gross Churn | >5% monthly | Product-market fit issues |
| NRR | <100% | Shrinking customer base |
| Burn Multiple | >3.0 | Inefficient growth |
| Runway | <12 months | Financing pressure |
| Customer Concentration | >25% top customer | Revenue risk |
