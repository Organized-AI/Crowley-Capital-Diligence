# Data Room Templates Skill

Generate professional due diligence outputs.

## Output Types

### 1. Executive Summary (1-Pager)
Quick reference for initial partner discussion.
- Company overview
- Investment thesis (3 bullets)
- Key metrics table
- Risk summary (top 3)
- Recommendation

### 2. Investment Memo
Comprehensive analysis for partner meeting.
- Company & market overview
- Business model & unit economics
- Team assessment
- Competitive landscape
- Risk assessment (11-risks)
- Deal terms & recommendation

### 3. Metrics Dashboard
Visual summary with charts and comparisons.

### 4. Risk Scorecard
11-risks visual assessment.
